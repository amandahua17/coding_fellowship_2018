Alia

Alia is a rising junior majoring in Sociology and Women, Gender & Sexuality Studies at WashU. She has been attempting to complete a research project on policing across the many municipalities in the St. Louis area, but did not know how to create a program that would help her sort through the data she collected. She found the Less Annoying CRM fellowship as an avenue through which she could develop a transferable skill and actually accomplishing her research goal.
