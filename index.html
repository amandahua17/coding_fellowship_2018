Coding Fellows

Alia

Alia is a rising junior majoring in Sociology and Women, Gender & Sexuality Studies at WashU. She has been attempting to complete a research project on policing across the many municipalities in the St. Louis area, but did not know how to create a program that would help her sort through the data she collected. She found the Less Annoying CRM fellowship as an avenue through which she could develop a transferable skill and actually accomplishing her research goal.


Amanda

Amanda is a rising sophomore majoring in Computer Science at WashU. Originally from Cupertino, California, Amanda spends her free time listening to music, watching netflix, playing volleyball, and reading. Her goal this summer is to learn as much about web development as possible and incorporate it into the creation of a website that will congregate resources for anyone who is stressed or anxious.


Elly

Elly is a rising junior at WashU majoring in Cognitive Neuroscience, and comes to LACRM from Atlanta, Georgia. This summer, she's looking forward to get out of WashU and explore STL's food scene!
